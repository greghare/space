A Space flight trivia game

HOW TO PLAY

Answer trivia questions at each planet.
If you're correct your fuel goes up 5%.
If you're incorrect you lose 10%.
Fuel runs out when you're not answering questions.

CONTROLS

W: Fly forwards | S: Fly backwards

A: Move left | D: Move right

Up: Pitch + | Down: Pitch -

Left: Yaw left | Right: Yaw right

Q: Roll left | E: Roll right

R: Up | F: Down

P: Play/Pause

Press F5 to try again
